# E-Commerce Sales Performance Dashboard (Power BI)

## Overview

This project presents an interactive Power BI dashboard developed to analyze e-commerce sales data and generate meaningful business insights. The dashboard focuses on revenue trends, customer behavior, product performance, and order distribution.

It enables stakeholders to explore data dynamically and supports data-driven decision-making.

---

## Objectives

* Analyze overall sales performance
* Identify top-performing product categories
* Understand customer purchasing behavior
* Track revenue trends over time
* Evaluate order status distribution

---

## Key Features

* Total Revenue: Overall sales generated
* Total Orders: Number of orders placed
* Average Order Value (AOV): Revenue per order
* Orders per Customer: Customer purchasing behavior
* Monthly Revenue Trend: Time-based analysis of sales
* Revenue by Product Category: Identification of top categories
* Revenue by City: Geographic distribution of revenue
* Order Status Distribution: Breakdown of order outcomes
* Interactive Filters: Dynamic filtering by date, category, and status

---

## Key Insights

* Revenue is primarily driven by delivered orders
* Peak sales occur during mid-year months
* A small number of product categories contribute the majority of revenue
* Order cancellations have a limited but noticeable impact on overall performance
